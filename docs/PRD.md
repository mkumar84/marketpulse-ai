# MarketPulse AI PRD

## Vision
Create a GenAI-powered alternative data intelligence platform that helps capital markets users detect emerging market signals, summarize drivers, and generate client-ready insights.

## MVP Users
- Equity research analyst
- Institutional sales
- Product strategist
- Capital markets innovation team

## Core Problem
Capital markets teams process fragmented signals from news, market data, filings, social discussion, and macro events. The workflow is manual and time-consuming.

## MVP Capabilities
- Alternative data ingestion
- Sentiment scoring
- RAG copilot
- Executive summary generation
- Evidence and governance layer

## Success Metrics
- Insight generation time under 30 seconds
- Source-grounded response quality
- Analyst time saved
- Adoption and repeat usage

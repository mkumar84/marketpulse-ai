# MarketPulse AI — UX Redesign Notes

## Design Direction
The UI is redesigned as a minimalist capital-markets command center rather than a generic Streamlit dashboard.

## Human-Centric Design Principles

1. **Executive-first hierarchy**
   - The first screen answers: What sector? What posture? What score? What evidence?
   - Detailed exploration is moved into a separate Signals Explorer.

2. **Progressive disclosure**
   - Users see summary metrics first.
   - Evidence, raw data, RAG admin, and market data are available only when needed.

3. **Explainability by default**
   - Every insight is connected to sources, themes, risks, and scores.
   - The UI supports human-in-the-loop validation rather than blind automation.

4. **Minimal visual language**
   - White cards, muted borders, subtle shadows, and reduced clutter.
   - Uses colour only for meaning: bullish, bearish, neutral, theme, risk.

5. **Role-oriented navigation**
   - Command Center: executives and product leaders.
   - Signals Explorer: analysts.
   - Research Copilot: conversational intelligence.
   - Executive Brief: client-ready output.
   - RAG Admin: technical demo capability.

## Suggested Interview Positioning
This redesign demonstrates that the product is not just technically functional. It is designed around capital markets workflows, trust, explainability, analyst review, and executive decision-making.

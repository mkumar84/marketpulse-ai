# Demo Script

## Opening
Capital markets teams face information overload across news, social signals, filings, macro data, and price movement. MarketPulse AI converts fragmented alternative data into explainable market intelligence.

## Walkthrough
1. Select AI Infrastructure.
2. Show sentiment score and top themes.
3. Highlight risks such as GPU supply, energy availability, and infrastructure bottlenecks.
4. Open source evidence.
5. Index the data into ChromaDB.
6. Ask the copilot: What are the top risks in AI infrastructure?
7. Generate a client-ready executive note.

## Close
This prototype demonstrates product strategy, GenAI, alternative data, RAG, explainability, and enterprise governance thinking.
